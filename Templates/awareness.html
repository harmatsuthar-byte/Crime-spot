General Safety Tips

  • Avoid walking alone late at night in poorly lit areas.
  • Always lock your vehicle and avoid leaving valuables visible inside.
  • Report any suspicious behavior to local authorities immediately.
  • Keep emergency contact numbers saved and accessible.
  • Educate children about personal safety and trusted adults.
